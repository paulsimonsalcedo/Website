Destination

Capas National Shrine commemorates the end of the Bataan Death March during World War 2, and the end of thousands of lives of Filipinos, Americans, and some Czech soldiers at the hands of the Japanese. A 70-meter or more than 220-feet obelisk or monument stands at the center of the park, surrounded by walls with which names of the known tortured/slain soldiers were engraved. Other details are also engraved, along with prayers, poems, and honorary recognitions.

Monasterio De Tarlac is another park, situated atop a mountain in San Jose, which has the famous 30-foot Jesus Christ Statue. The park is open to public, where tourists can enjoy sightseeing activities or attend a religious mass in its churches.
