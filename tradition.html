Tradition

Tarlac gets along with its neighbor Pampanga very much that somehow they share beliefs and approaches towards them. If the City of San Fernando is known for its "Giant Lantern Festival" during Christmas Season, Tarlac holds its annual "Belenismo Festival," from which the city, along with municipalities, compete in a colorful showdown of "Nativity Scene" or "The Feast of the Birth of Jesus."

Tarlac also celebrates its diversity. The annual "Tarlac Kanlahi Festival" highlights diverse cultures from one municipality to another, and it showcases crafts, cuisine, and the most-awaited beauty pageant.
